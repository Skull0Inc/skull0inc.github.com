Welcome to Skull0Inc

Aug 27th, 2012

This is my (Skull0Inc) introductory post for testing the use of the [octopress] blogging framework which was used to to generate these website pages and along with the hosting of the site which is being done by [github].

For some time now I have always wanted to have a website just for the sake of being part of what the internet is -which is a collection of human shared ideas- as we collectively assimilate our disparate thoughts into harnessing the also ever growing collective power of the internet. Now it is more of a means of necessity that I think I should have a website. For me, it is going to be a personal documentation area, where I can just jot down any procedures or things that I come across ever-so-often so that they may be like little reminders of things that I think are noteworthy. Also for you, hopefully it will be a resource of little reminders . In addition to that, its just a fun creative outlet for me to do something somewhat technical and enjoy the use of technology as it is in today’s format. Yes, that’s it. Its a celebration of the internet by using that very said collective resource and adding to it.
